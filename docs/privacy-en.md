# Privacy Policy — Luckura

**Last updated: June 2026**

Luckura ("we", "our", or "the app") is a luck-tools application. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app.

## Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.** Luckura does not require an account, does not track users, and does not transmit any information to external servers.

## Local Storage

The app saves your preferences (names, dice count, team count, language, theme) locally on your device using the browser's localStorage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

## Third-Party Services

Luckura does not use any analytics, advertising, or tracking services. No third party receives any data about you or your usage.

## Internet Access

The app works fully offline. Internet access is not required after the initial download.

## Children

Luckura does not knowingly collect any data from children or any other users, as it collects no data at all.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
