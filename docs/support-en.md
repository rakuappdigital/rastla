## Frequently Asked Questions

**Does the app require an internet connection?**
No. Luckura works fully offline after installation.

**Is my data stored on any server?**
No. All preferences and names are stored only on your device and are never sent anywhere.

**How do I switch between Turkish and English?**
Tap the TR / EN toggle in the top-right corner of the app.

**How do I reset my data?**
On any page, tap the "Reset" button at the top right. You will be asked to confirm before anything is deleted.

**The app is not working correctly. What should I do?**
Try closing and reopening the app. If the problem persists, contact us at the email above with a description of the issue and your device model.

**Can I add more than 16 names to the Raffle jar?**
The jar displays up to 16 names at once for visual clarity, but the draw is performed from the full list you enter.
